# IPMS Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD)

This ERD describes the relational model for the Intelligent Project Monitoring System based on the approved documentation.

## Entities

- `User`
- `Role`
- `UserRole`
- `Department`
- `Cohort`
- `StudentProfile`
- `SupervisorProfile`
- `AdminProfile`
- `Project`
- `ProjectAssignment`
- `Milestone`
- `Submission`
- `SubmissionReview`
- `DiscussionThread`
- `DiscussionMessage`
- `Notification`
- `AnalyticsSnapshot`
- `AIHealthScore`
- `AIRiskSignal`
- `AIRecommendation`
- `Forecast`
- `Report`
- `AuditLog`

## Relationship Summary

- A `User` can have multiple `Role` values through `UserRole`.
- A `User` may be linked to one `StudentProfile`, `SupervisorProfile`, and/or `AdminProfile`.
- A `Department` manages `User`s, `Cohort`s, `Project`s, and `AdminProfile`s.
- A `Cohort` holds `StudentProfile`s and `Project`s.
- A `StudentProfile` can own multiple `Project`s and `Submission`s.
- A `SupervisorProfile` can oversee multiple `Project`s.
- A `Project` is associated to one student and one supervisor, plus optional department and cohort.
- `ProjectAssignment` allows multiple people to be assigned to a project in different roles.
- A `Project` contains `Milestone`s, `Submission`s, `DiscussionThread`s, `Notification`s, `AnalyticsSnapshot`s, AI outputs, and `Report`s.
- A `Milestone` can have many `Submission`s.
- A `Submission` may have multiple `SubmissionReview`s.
- A `DiscussionThread` contains `DiscussionMessage`s, which may nest replies.
- `Notification`s target `User`s and can optionally reference a `Project`.
- AI outputs (`AIHealthScore`, `AIRiskSignal`, `AIRecommendation`, `Forecast`) are tied to a `Project`.
- `Report`s may target a project, supervisor, cohort, or department, and are generated by a `User`.
- `AuditLog` captures user actions against entities.

## Index and Constraint Notes

- `User.email` is unique for authentication and lookup.
- `Department.code` and `StudentProfile.enrollmentId` are unique.
- `ProjectAssignment` ensures a user cannot have duplicate roles on the same project.
- Many lookup tables are indexed by `projectId`, `recipientId`, `supervisorId`, and `createdAt`.
- Soft deletes are modeled by optional `deletedAt` fields where audit history is required.

## Suggested ERD Layout

1. `User` at the center, connected to `UserRole`, `StudentProfile`, `SupervisorProfile`, `AdminProfile`, `Project`, `Submission`, `Notification`, `Report`, and `AuditLog`.
2. `Project` connected to `Milestone`, `Submission`, `DiscussionThread`, AI outputs, `Notification`, `AnalyticsSnapshot`, and `Report`.
3. `DiscussionThread` connected to `DiscussionMessage`.
4. `Submission` connected to `SubmissionReview`.
5. `Department` and `Cohort` provide organizational grouping for users and projects.
